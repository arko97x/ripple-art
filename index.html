About Ripple-Art


Ripple-Art is an-almost weekend project by Arkoprabho Bhattacharjee (arccc.co).

It's meant to be a canvas that promotes the creation of art (read: I was bored out of my mind over the weekend).

But, in all honesty, this was definitely a lot of fun to build. Hope you have fun playing around with it too.

Tap/click away!

P.S: Best enjoyed on a desktop.
P.P.S: Click/tap multiple times for multiple ripples!
